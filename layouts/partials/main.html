Here is a couple of my apps:

Anki Auto Cards is a tool for iOS that can simplify the process of creating new flashcards by automatically generating cards based on user input. With this tool, users could save time and increase their efficiency when creating Anki cards on their iOS devices, making the learning process smoother and more effective.
GCash Mobile app can enable users to manage their personal finances on the go. The app could allow users to connect to their GNU Cash databases, view account balances, create and edit transactions. By offering an intuitive and easy-to-use interface for managing finances, the app could help users stay organized and in control of their money, no matter where they are.
